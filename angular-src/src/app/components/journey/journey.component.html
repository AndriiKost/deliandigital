elian Gathering

We will ask lots of questions, to understand you vision on the product, and adapt to your business and your team.

Business requirements are gathered in this phase.

As a result, We will create a Requirement Specification document, which will serve the purpose of guideline for the future steps.

elian Design

In this phase the software design is prepared from the requirement specifications.

Our designers will do their best creative work, to show how we understood your vision we will start from designing wireframes. Then, after your approval, We will move to mockup stage of Delian Design.

This mockup will be a completed design version of your product, and a design guideline for developers.

Also the testers comes up with the Test strategy, where they mention what to test, how to test.

elian Code

After receiving system design documents, the work will be divided in modules/units and actual coding is started.

This is the longest phase of the Delian Journey, with a main focus on the developers.

After the code is developed it is tested against the requirements to make sure that the product is actually solving the needs addressed and gathered during the Delian Requirements Gathering.

elian Delivery

After successful testing the product is delivered / deployed to the customer for their use.

As soon as the product is given to the customers they will first do the beta testing. If any changes are required or if any bugs are caught, then they will report it to the engineering team.

Once those changes are made or the bugs are fixed then the final deployment will happen.
